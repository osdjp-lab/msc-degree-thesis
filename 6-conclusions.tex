\section{Conclusions}\label{Conclusions}

In this paper it was found that for a sample window of 30 days the selected dataset and timeframe allow for the prediction of the exchange rate of the \gls{usd} on the basis of the \gls{nzd}, \gls{sek}, \gls{cad}, \gls{dkk} and \gls{pln}.

The model with the highest accuracy, \gls{svr}, had a \gls{mape} of 1.524\% and the lowest, the \gls{mlp} had an \gls{mape} of 2.96\%. As such even in the worst case the models were capable of accurate prediction of the fluctuations of the \gls{usd} exchange rate. 

In conclusion all of the models used in this paper exhibit significant predictive potential of use in the area of currency exchange rate forecasting.
