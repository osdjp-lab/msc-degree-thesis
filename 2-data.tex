\section{Data}\label{Data}

\subsection{Datasets}\label{Acquisition}

The data chosen for this paper was obtained from the European Central Bank ECB Data Portal. It consists of the quarterly bilateral exchange rates from 1999Q1 to 2025Q1 of the following currency pairs: 

- Swiss franc/Euro [CHF/EUR]
- Czech koruna/Euro [CZK/EUR]
- Danish krone/Euro [DKK/EUR]
- UK pound sterling/Euro [GBP/EUR]
- Japanese yen/Euro [JPY/EUR]
- Polish zloty/Euro [PLN/EUR]
- Swedish krona/Euro [SEK/EUR]
- US dollar/Euro [USD/EUR]
